Why most solar setups disappoint

More panels isn't more power. Balance is.

Undercharged batteries — too many panels for too little storage means power generated is never properly captured.

System shutdowns — inverters that can't handle peak load trip off exactly when you need them most.

Shortened equipment lifespan — components forced outside their design range wear out years early.

Poor return on investment — a well-designed 6kVA system can outperform a poorly planned 10kVA one.
