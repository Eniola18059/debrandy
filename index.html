Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

Straight answers to what most people ask before they commit to a system. Still unsure? Message us on WhatsApp and we'll walk you through it.

A good lithium (LiFePO4) battery typically lasts several years and thousands of charge cycles when it's sized correctly and not overworked. Lifespan depends heavily on how the system is designed — an undersized battery pushed beyond its limits daily will wear out much faster than one matched properly to your load.

Yes, but it needs proper sizing. ACs draw a heavy startup load, so the inverter and battery bank both need enough headroom to handle that spike without tripping. This is exactly the kind of detail we check during a load assessment before recommending a system.

It depends entirely on what you're running and how much of it runs at the same time. A small apartment might only need 1–3kVA, while a shop, hotel, or factory could need anywhere from 10kVA to well over 100kW. Rather than guess, message us your appliance list and we'll size it properly.

Panel count is driven by your battery capacity and how much sunlight you get, not by guesswork. We calculate the panel-to-storage ratio so your panels charge your battery optimally — enough to keep up with your daily usage without oversupplying or underperforming.

Yes — several of our systems, including the SAKO Solar Generator, are built to be expandable. If you're planning to scale up in stages, tell us upfront so we can design the initial setup in a way that supports adding more battery capacity or panels down the line.

Warranty terms depend on the specific brand and component, since each manufacturer sets its own coverage. We'll confirm the exact warranty for your chosen equipment before you buy, and every battery is inspected for performance and safety before it leaves us.

A straightforward home installation can often be completed within a day once the system has been designed and components are on-site. Larger commercial or industrial jobs take longer depending on scale. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your consultation.
